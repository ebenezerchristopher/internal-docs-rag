# Record Retention

## Schedule
- BOLs and packing lists: 7 years.
- Customs entries: 5 years.
- Hazmat shipping papers: 2 years after the material is shipped.
- Driver qualification files: 3 years after the driver leaves.
- Insurance certificates: 7 years after expiration.
- Client MSAs: 7 years after termination.

## Storage
Active records are in the TMS, WMS, and document management system.
Archived records are in cold storage with a documented retrieval SLA.

## Deletion
Records past the retention period are deleted on a quarterly cadence.
The deletion is logged.

## Legal hold
If a record is under legal hold, deletion is suspended for the affected
records until the hold is released by Legal.
