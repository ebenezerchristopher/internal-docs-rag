# Data Privacy

## Scope
This policy applies to all personal data we process: client contacts,
driver records, employee records, and tracking data that can be linked
to an individual.

## Principles
- Lawful basis: we collect personal data only with a documented basis
  (contract, consent, legitimate interest).
- Data minimization: we collect only what we need.
- Retention: personal data is retained per the retention schedule and
  then deleted.
- Security: encryption in transit and at rest; access on a need-to-know
  basis.

## Client data
Client contacts and shipment data are processed under the MSA. We do not
sell client data. We do not share client data with third parties except
as required to provide the service (carriers, brokers) or as required by
law.

## Breach response
A suspected breach is reported to the Compliance Lead within 1 hour.
The Compliance Lead notifies the leadership team and, where required,
the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours.
