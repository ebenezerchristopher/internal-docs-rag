# Export Controls

## EAR / ITAR
- EAR-controlled items (most dual-use commercial goods) require an ECCN
  and may require a license to specific destinations or end-users.
- ITAR-controlled items (defense articles and services) require a USML
  category and a license for any export.

## Sanctions screening
Every counterparty (consignee, end-user, beneficial owner) is screened
against the OFAC SDN list, the EU sanctions list, and the UK sanctions
list before booking. Screening is re-run on every shipment; we do not
rely on annual screening.

## Restricted party lists
We do not transact with parties on any restricted party list, including
the US BIS Entity List, the US BIS Denied Persons List, and the US
Department of State Debarred List.

## Recordkeeping
Export records are kept for 5 years from the date of export.
