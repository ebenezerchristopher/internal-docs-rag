# Hazmat Handling

## Training
Every employee who handles, offers, or transports hazmat must complete
the appropriate DOT/IATA/IMDG training within the last 3 years. Records
are kept in the LMS.

## Classification
- Confirm the UN number, proper shipping name, hazard class, and packing
  group before quoting.
- For mixed classes, confirm segregation with the safety data sheet.
- When in doubt, escalate to the Compliance Lead; do not guess.

## Documentation
- Shipper's declaration (IATA) or hazmat BOL (ground).
- Emergency response information attached to the freight.
- Marking and labeling per the modal regulation.

## What not to do
- Do not accept undeclared hazmat. The fines are personal, not just
  corporate.
- Do not overpack incompatible classes.
- Do not quote hazmat without disclosing the surcharge.
