# Insurance Requirements

## Company policies
- General liability: $2M per occurrence, $5M aggregate.
- Cargo: $250K per occurrence (or higher for specific commodities).
- Auto: $1M CSL for company vehicles; carriers must carry $1M CSL.
- Workers' comp: statutory limits.
- Cyber: $5M per occurrence.

## Carrier and broker evidence
Every carrier and broker on the panel must provide a certificate of
insurance evidencing the above minimums (auto and cargo for carriers;
professional liability and cyber for brokers). Certificates are renewed
annually and tracked in the carrier management system.

## Client cargo
Clients may declare higher cargo values; the company carries
contingent cargo coverage up to $5M per occurrence. Declarations above
that require Director approval and may incur a surcharge.
