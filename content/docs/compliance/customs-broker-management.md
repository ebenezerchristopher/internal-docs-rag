# Customs Broker Management

## Selection
We use a panel of licensed brokers by region. The panel is reviewed
annually. Brokers are scored on: clearance time, first-time-right rate,
communication, ACE/ACI/ACI eManifest compliance.

## Handoff
- For inbound ocean/air, the broker is identified at the time of booking.
- For outbound, the broker is the destination country's broker of record;
  the client may nominate their own with 48 hours' notice.

## Bond
The company maintains a continuous customs bond sufficient for the rolling
90-day import volume. The bond is reviewed quarterly.

## Audit
Every quarter, Compliance pulls a random sample of 25 entries and
reconciles the entered values, classifications, and duty payments against
the underlying documents. Errors are tracked and fed back to the broker.
