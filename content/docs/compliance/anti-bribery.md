# Anti-Bribery and Anti-Corruption

## Policy
We do not offer, pay, solicit, or accept bribes. This applies to
government officials and to private counterparties. Facilitation
payments are prohibited except where there is a credible threat to
life, limb, or liberty, and even then only with Director approval.

## Gifts and hospitality
- Under $100 per item, infrequent, and not in cash: allowed.
- Over $100 or frequent: requires Compliance review.
- Never given to a government official without Compliance pre-approval.

## Third parties
Carriers, brokers, and other third parties are subject to our third-party
code of conduct. We do not retain third parties that do not accept the
code.

## Reporting
Suspected violations are reported to the Compliance Lead or via the
anonymous hotline. Retaliation against good-faith reporters is
prohibited.
