# Driver Onboarding

## Pre-hire
- Application, MVR review, PSP review, and a road test.
- DOT physical (current within 2 years) and a negative pre-employment
  drug screen.
- Reference checks: 3 previous employers, including the most recent
  carrier.

## First day
- Issue uniforms, badge, and handheld.
- Walk-through of the cab, the ELD, and the routing app.
- Review the driver handbook; sign the acknowledgment.

## First 30 days
- Ride-along on at least 3 shifts.
- Weekly check-in with the driver manager.
- Confirm the CDL endorsement for any hazmat the driver will haul.

## First 90 days
- Quarterly review of the MVR, ELD logs, and any incidents.
- Driver is moved to standard cadence at day 90 unless issues persist.
