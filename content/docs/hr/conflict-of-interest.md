# Conflict of Interest

## Disclosures
Employees disclose, in writing, any: outside employment, ownership
interest in a supplier or competitor, family relationship with a
supplier or competitor, or other relationship that could influence
their judgment on behalf of the company.

## Timing
- On hire.
- Annually, on the conflict of interest questionnaire.
- Within 30 days of any new conflict arising.

## Review
Disclosures are reviewed by HR and, where the relationship is
material, by the leadership team. A mitigation plan is documented
(typically recusal from related decisions).

## Failure to disclose
Failure to disclose a known conflict is grounds for termination.
