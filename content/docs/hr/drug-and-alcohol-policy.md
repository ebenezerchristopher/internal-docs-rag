# Drug and Alcohol Policy

## Testing
- Pre-employment: required for all drivers.
- Random: 25% of drivers per year for alcohol, 50% for drugs.
- Post-accident: required for any accident meeting the DOT threshold.
- Reasonable suspicion: required when a supervisor has articulable
  suspicion.
- Return-to-duty and follow-up: required after a positive result.

## Substances
Prohibited substances are per the DOT 49 CFR Part 40 panel. Medical
marijuana is treated as prohibited for safety-sensitive positions.

## Consequences
A positive result or refusal is grounds for removal from the
safety-sensitive position. The driver may complete a SAP-led
return-to-duty process and reapply after 90 days.

## Confidentiality
Results are kept in a confidential medical file, separate from the
personnel file, with access restricted to HR and the SAP.
