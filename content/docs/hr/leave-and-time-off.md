# Leave and Time Off

## PTO
- 15 days/year for years 0-2.
- 20 days/year for years 3-5.
- 25 days/year for years 6+.
- Accrual capped at 1.5x the annual entitlement; use-it-or-lose-it
  beyond the cap.

## Holidays
11 paid holidays per year: New Year's Day, MLK Day, Presidents' Day,
Memorial Day, Juneteenth, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving
(plus day after), Christmas Eve, Christmas Day, New Year's Eve.

## Leaves
- FMLA: 12 weeks unpaid, job-protected, per the federal statute.
- State family leave: as required by state law.
- Bereavement: up to 5 days for immediate family, 2 for extended.
- Jury duty: as required.

## Request process
All leave requests go through the HRIS. Approval is required before
the leave begins, except in emergencies.
