# Remote Work Policy

## Eligibility
Most non-driving roles are eligible for hybrid (2-3 days in office) or
fully remote, subject to manager approval. Driving roles are not
eligible for remote work; the office is the cab.

## Expectations
- Available during core hours (10am-4pm local).
- Responsive in the team chat within 30 minutes during work hours.
- Camera-on for client-facing meetings unless the client prefers
  otherwise.
- Respectful of time zones when scheduling cross-region meetings.

## Equipment
- Company laptop, monitor, keyboard, mouse.
- $500 stipend for home office setup, one-time.
- $50/month internet stipend.
- Replacement cadence: laptop every 3 years, monitor every 5 years.

## Security
VPN on for any access to internal systems. No working from public
Wi-Fi without VPN.
