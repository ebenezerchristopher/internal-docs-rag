# Driver Handbook Acknowledgment

## What drivers sign
The driver handbook covers: hours of service, ELD use, drug and alcohol
policy, accident reporting, customer interaction, dress code, pay and
benefits, grievance procedure, and the company's anti-harassment policy.

## How often
On hire, on any update, and annually. The acknowledgment is kept in the
driver's personnel file for 3 years after the driver leaves.

## Updates
Material updates are communicated in writing and acknowledged
individually. Minor updates (typos, formatting) are communicated
collectively.

## Refusal to sign
A driver who refuses to sign an updated handbook is suspended pending
HR review. Continued refusal is grounds for termination.
