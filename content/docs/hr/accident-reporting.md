# Accident Reporting

## What counts
Any event involving a company vehicle that results in: a fatality, an
injury requiring medical treatment beyond first aid, a vehicle
disabling damage, or property damage over $1,000.

## Driver steps at the scene
1. Stop, secure the scene, and check for injuries.
2. Call 911 if there are injuries or if local law requires it.
3. Exchange information with the other party.
4. Photograph the scene, the vehicles, and the damage.
5. Do not admit fault or discuss the accident with anyone other than
   police, the company's insurance, and the driver manager.

## Reporting timeline
- Call the driver manager immediately.
- Submit the accident report within 4 hours.
- Submit the drug and alcohol test within 32 hours (post-accident).
- Insurance claim filed within 24 hours.

## Post-accident review
Every accident is reviewed by the safety committee within 14 days.
Findings are documented and fed back into training.
