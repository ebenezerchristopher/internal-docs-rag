# Performance Review Cycle

## Cadence
- Quarterly check-in for all employees.
- Annual formal review for all employees.
- Drivers: in-cab ride-along at least once per year.

## Inputs
- Self-assessment.
- Manager assessment.
- Peer feedback (360 for managers and above).
- Quantified metrics where applicable (e.g., on-time delivery for
  drivers, NPS for account owners).

## Calibration
Reviews are calibrated across teams to reduce manager-level bias. The
calibration is led by HR and the leadership team.

## Outcomes
- Exceeds: considered for promotion and increased scope.
- Meets: continue on the current path.
- Below: performance improvement plan with a 60-90 day window.

## Documentation
Reviews are kept in the personnel file for the duration of employment
plus 3 years.
