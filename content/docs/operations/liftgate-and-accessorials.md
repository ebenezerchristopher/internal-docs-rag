# Liftgate and Accessorials

## When to add accessorials at quote time
- Liftgate: residential or limited-access delivery.
- Inside delivery: client request, in writing.
- Sort and segregate: orders for multiple SKUs to multiple departments.
- Hazmat: any shipment carrying hazmat, regardless of class.
- Re-consignment or re-delivery: only with Ops approval.

## Confirmation
The accessorial must be on the quote and on the BOL. Accessorials added
after the fact are at the carrier's discretion and often not honored.

## Billing
Accessorials are billed as separate line items. The carrier invoice and the
client invoice must reconcile to the penny; if they do not, escalate to
Finance.
