# Warehouse Shipping

## Pick and pack
- Pull orders by WMS print; do not pick from memory.
- Verify SKU, quantity, and lot/serial against the pick ticket.
- Pack to the carrier's published requirements; do not over-pack parcel.
- Apply the carrier label and the SSCC pallet label as applicable.

## Load planning
- Stage outbound freight by carrier and stop.
- Confirm weight and dimensions against the BOL before the driver arrives.
- Re-weigh any shipment that is within 5% of a weight break.

## Driver arrival
1. Driver checks in at the security gate.
2. Warehouse loads; driver counts pallets and pieces.
3. Driver signs the BOL; warehouse keeps a copy.
4. Freight is scanned out of the WMS at the time of load.
