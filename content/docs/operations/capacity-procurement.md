# Capacity Procurement

## Lane tiers
- Tier 1: contracted, dedicated capacity. Used for 80%+ of the volume.
- Tier 2: contracted, flexible capacity. Used to absorb surges up to 20%
  above the contract.
- Spot: ad-hoc. Used only when Tier 1 and Tier 2 cannot cover.

## Spot rules
- Always get a second quote on shipments over $5,000.
- Never accept a spot rate more than 30% above the contracted lane rate
  without Director approval; it is usually cheaper to wait for tomorrow.
- Always check the carrier's safety rating and insurance before booking spot.

## Carrier scorecard
Each carrier is reviewed quarterly on: on-time pickup, on-time delivery,
claims ratio, communication. Carriers scoring below 70% for two quarters
are removed from the panel.
