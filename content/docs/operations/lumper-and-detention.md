# Lumper and Detention

## Lumper fees
A lumper is a third party hired to unload freight. Lumper fees are common
at grocery and retail receivers.

- Always confirm the fee in advance with the receiver.
- Always get a receipt.
- Always pass the receipt to billing for invoicing; the client pays, we do
  not absorb.

## Detention
Detention is the charge the carrier levies when loading or unloading takes
longer than the free window (typically 2 hours).

- Free time starts when the driver arrives, not when the driver is checked
  in.
- For repeated detention at a specific receiver, escalate to the receiver
  and adjust the schedule.

## Driver waits
If our own driver is waiting at a customer location, the wait is billable
to the customer at the published hourly rate.
