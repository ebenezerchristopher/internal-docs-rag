# On-Call Procedures

## Rotation
Weekly rotation, Monday 09:00 to Monday 09:00. Primary and secondary on-call
for each region (NA, EU, APAC). The on-call person must be reachable within
15 minutes for the entire week.

## What counts as a page
- Any client escalation that has not been answered within 1 business hour.
- A shipment exception exceeding $5,000 in client impact.
- A safety incident involving a company driver or contracted asset.
- A systems outage affecting booking, tracking, or invoicing.

## What does not count as a page
- Routine client questions (handle in normal business hours).
- Carrier paperwork errors (handle next business day).
- Internal IT issues not affecting clients.

## After a page
- Acknowledge within 15 minutes.
- Resolve or escalate within 1 hour.
- File a short incident note in the on-call log the next business day.
