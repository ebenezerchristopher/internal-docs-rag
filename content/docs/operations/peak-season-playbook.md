# Peak Season Playbook

## Trigger dates
- Q4 retail peak: November 1 to December 23.
- Chinese New Year: 4 weeks before to 2 weeks after.
- Produce season: May 1 to October 31 (varies by region).

## Pre-peak (T-30)
- Confirm contracted capacity with all Tier 1 carriers.
- Pre-book seasonal equipment (reefer, dry van surge).
- Confirm holiday schedules with the major carriers.

## During peak
- Daily ops standup moves to 06:00 and 18:00.
- Spot capacity is allowed up to 15% above the contracted rate without
  approval.
- Client ETAs are given with a 1-day buffer; the buffer is communicated
  up-front.

## Post-peak
- Reconcile any remaining invoices within 30 days.
- Debrief with each Tier 1 carrier within 14 days.
- File a post-peak report to the leadership team.
