# Warehouse Receiving

## Pre-arrival
- Confirm the BOL and PO 24 hours before the scheduled arrival.
- Stage the receiving area; print the receiving report.
- For blind receipts (no PO), flag for QA spot-check on receipt.

## On arrival
1. Driver checks in at the security gate.
2. Warehouse counts pallets, packages, and pieces against the BOL.
3. Driver signs the BOL; warehouse signs the receiving copy.
4. Discrepancies are noted on the BOL and photographed.
5. Freight is scanned into the WMS within 1 hour of unload.

## Discrepancies
- Visible damage: refuse the freight, photograph, and call Ops.
- Concealed damage: open within 24 hours, photograph, and call Ops.
- Shortage: note on BOL, photograph the trailer, and call Ops.
- Overage: refuse unless the client has approved receipt of overages in
  writing.
