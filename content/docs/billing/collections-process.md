# Collections Process

## Cadence
- Day 1 past due: friendly reminder email.
- Day 7 past due: phone call to the AP contact.
- Day 14 past due: written demand; account flagged in CRM.
- Day 30 past due: hold on new bookings pending payment plan.
- Day 60 past due: escalation to legal.

## Payment plans
Available for clients in good standing with a documented short-term
issue. Plans are written, signed, and tracked. Defaults trigger
re-hold.

## Disputes
Disputed invoices are removed from collections while the dispute is
open. The dispute must be filed within 14 days of the invoice date.

## Writing off
Bad debt is written off only with VP approval. The write-off is
documented with the underlying cause.
