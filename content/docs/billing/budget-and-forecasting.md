# Budget and Forecasting

## Annual budget
Drafted in October for the following year. Approved by the
leadership team in November. Board review in December.

## Quarterly reforecast
The leadership team reforecasts in month 2 of each quarter. The
reforecast is compared to the original budget and the prior
reforecast; variances over 5% are explained in the reforecast
narrative.

## Monthly close
The books are closed by the 10th business day of the following
month. The close includes: bank rec, AR aging, AP aging, accruals,
and intercompany.

## Reporting
- Weekly: cash position, AR aging.
- Monthly: P&L, balance sheet, cash flow, KPI dashboard.
- Quarterly: full reforecast, board package.
