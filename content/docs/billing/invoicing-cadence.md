# Invoicing Cadence

## Standard
- Invoices are issued on the day the freight is delivered (or the
  service is rendered).
- Statements are issued on the 1st and 15th of each month.

## Payment terms
- Net 30 for Standard tier.
- Net 15 for Premier tier.
- Net 60 only with Director approval and a credit review.

## Currency
Invoiced in the currency of the contract. Conversion is at the
mid-market rate on the invoice date.

## What goes on the invoice
- Freight charge.
- Accessorials (each as a separate line).
- Fuel surcharge, calculated per the contracted methodology.
- Applicable taxes.
- Any credit memos applied.

## What does not go on the invoice
- Carrier invoice copies (kept on file, not attached).
- Internal notes or comments.
