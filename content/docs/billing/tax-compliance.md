# Tax Compliance

## Sales tax
- US sales tax: applied per the destination state and the client's
  tax-exempt status.
- VAT (EU): applied per the destination country and the client's
  VAT number.
- GST (Canada, Australia, etc.): applied per the destination
  jurisdiction.

## Tax-exempt clients
Clients with a valid tax-exempt certificate are invoiced without
tax. The certificate is kept on file and re-validated annually.

## Reverse charge
For B2B cross-border transactions in the EU, the reverse charge
applies. The invoice notes "reverse charge" and omits VAT.

## Filings
- US sales tax: monthly or quarterly per the state's filing
  frequency.
- EU VAT: quarterly OSS return for B2C, periodic EC sales list for
  B2B.
- Income tax: filed annually by the company's accountants.
