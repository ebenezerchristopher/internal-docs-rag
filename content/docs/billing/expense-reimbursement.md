# Expense Reimbursement

## Eligible expenses
- Travel: economy airfare, standard hotel, rideshare, rental car at
  the mid-size tier or lower.
- Meals: up to $75/day domestic, $100/day international, with
  receipts.
- Client entertainment: pre-approved by the manager, with
  attendees and business purpose.
- Office supplies: pre-approved, with receipts.

## Submission
Expenses are submitted in the expense management tool within 30
days of incurring the expense. Receipts are required for any
expense over $25.

## Approval
- Under $500: manager approval.
- $500-$2,000: Director approval.
- Over $2,000: VP approval.

## Reimbursement
Reimbursement is on the next payroll cycle after approval. Direct
deposit only.
