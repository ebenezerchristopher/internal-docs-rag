# Carrier Pay

## Terms
Net 30 from receipt of a valid invoice. Quick-pay (7 days) is
available at a 2% discount, taken at the carrier's election.

## Required on the invoice
- BOL number.
- Pickup and delivery dates.
- Weight and dimensions.
- Accessorials (each as a separate line).
- Rate or reference to the contracted lane rate.

## Discrepancies
Discrepancies between the carrier invoice and the BOL are reconciled
with the carrier. Discrepancies over $500 are escalated to the Ops
team for resolution.

## 1099
Carriers are paid as 1099 contractors. The company files 1099-NEC
annually for any carrier paid more than $600 in the calendar year.
