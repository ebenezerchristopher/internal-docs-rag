# Warehouse Safety and PPE

## Required PPE
- Safety shoes (steel or composite toe) on the warehouse floor.
- High-visibility vest in any active loading area.
- Cut-resistant gloves when handling cardboard or shrink-wrap.
- Hearing protection in any area marked above 85 dB.

## Equipment operation
- Forklifts: certified operators only. Certification renewed every 3
  years.
- Pallet jacks: trained associates only.
- Conveyors: lockout/tagout before any maintenance.

## Housekeeping
- Aisle clearance: minimum 36 inches.
- Pallets: stacked no more than 2 high in the active area.
- Spills: cleaned up immediately; spills over 1 gallon are
  reported to the Manager.

## Incidents
Every incident (near-miss or actual) is reported within the same
shift. Trends are reviewed monthly.
