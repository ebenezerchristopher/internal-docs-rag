# Returns Processing

## RMA required
Every return requires a Return Material Authorization (RMA) with a
documented reason. No freight is accepted without an RMA.

## Receipt
1. Verify the RMA against the inbound freight.
2. Open and inspect the freight within 24 hours.
3. Grade the return: A (resellable as new), B (resellable as
   refurbished), C (scrap).

## Disposition
- A-grade: returned to stock.
- B-grade: routed to refurbishment or sold through the B-grade
  channel.
- C-grade: scrapped with documentation.

## Credits
Credits are issued within 5 business days of the return being
processed. The credit amount is per the RMA, not the original
invoice.
