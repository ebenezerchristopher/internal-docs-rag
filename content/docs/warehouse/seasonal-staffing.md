# Seasonal Staffing

## When
- Q4 retail peak: October 15 to January 15.
- Other peaks as needed (tax season, produce season, etc.).

## Sourcing
- Temp agencies on the panel, ranked by historical fit and quality.
- Direct hires for the seasonal peak are converted to full-time
  based on performance.

## Onboarding
- 2-hour safety orientation.
- On-the-job training for the specific function.
- Buddy system for the first week.

## Conversion
Top performers (top 20%) are offered full-time roles by week 6 of
the peak. Offers are made in writing with a start date and a
conversion bonus.
