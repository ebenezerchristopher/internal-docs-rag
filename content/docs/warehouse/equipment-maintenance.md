# Warehouse Equipment Maintenance

## Schedule
- Forklifts: full service every 250 hours, battery check weekly.
- Pallet jacks: lubrication monthly, full service every 500 hours.
- Conveyors: belt tension check weekly, full service quarterly.
- Dock doors: full service semi-annually.
- WMS hardware (scanners, printers): replacement on failure;
  preventative cleaning monthly.

## Spare parts
- Critical spares (forklift forks, pallet jack wheels, scanner
  batteries) are kept on hand.
- Reorder thresholds trigger automatic purchase orders.

## Downtime
Any equipment downtime affecting operations is reported immediately.
A backup plan is documented for each critical piece of equipment.
