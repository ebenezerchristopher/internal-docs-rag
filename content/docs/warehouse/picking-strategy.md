# Picking Strategy

## Method by order profile
- Single-line, single-unit orders: pick-to-tote, single picker.
- Multi-line, low-volume: pick-to-cart, single picker.
- Multi-line, high-volume: batch pick, multiple pickers, sort at
  pack.

## Slotting
- A-items (fast movers) at waist height near the pack station.
- B-items at lower or higher levels.
- C-items (slow movers) in the overflow area.

## Replenishment
Replenishment is triggered when a pick face drops below 25% of its
target. Replenishment is done during off-peak hours to avoid
interrupting picks.

## Accuracy
Target pick accuracy is 99.8%. Below 99.5% triggers a coaching
review. Recurring errors are escalated.
