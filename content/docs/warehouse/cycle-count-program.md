# Cycle Count Program

## Cadence
- A-count items (top 20% by value): counted weekly.
- B-count items (next 30% by value): counted monthly.
- C-count items (bottom 50% by value): counted quarterly.

## Method
- Counts are by SKU and lot/serial.
- Two-person counts for any item with a value over $1,000.
- Variance over 1% triggers a recount by a different associate.

## Discrepancies
- Inventory adjustment is made only after the recount confirms the
  variance.
- Adjustments over $5,000 require Manager approval.
- Patterns of variance (same SKU, same associate) trigger a coaching
  conversation.

## Annual full count
A full physical inventory is taken once per year, in the slow
season. The full count is audited by an external firm.
