# Rate Quote Guidelines

## When to quote
- Inbound requests with origin, destination, commodity, weight, dimensions,
  and pickup date are quotable.
- Requests missing any of the above are not quotable; reply asking for the
  missing data.

## Pricing inputs
- Use the contracted rate card for the lane and the client tier.
- Apply accessorials (liftgate, residential, inside delivery) only when
  confirmed by the client.
- For spot quotes above $5,000, get Ops sign-off before sending.

## Quote validity
Quotes are valid for 14 calendar days unless otherwise noted. The quote
document must include: rate, transit time, accessorials, fuel surcharge
methodology, and any caveats.

## What not to do
- Never quote a transit time shorter than the carrier's published minimum.
- Never quote a rate that is below the carrier's contracted minimum without
  Director approval.
- Never quote without noting hazmat surcharges when the commodity is hazmat.
