# Service Tier Comparison

## Standard
- 8x5 support (Mon-Fri, 8am-6pm local).
- Quotes within 4 business hours.
- Standard carrier liability ($100/lb cap).
- Web booking and tracking only.

## Plus
- 16x5 support (Mon-Fri, 6am-10pm local).
- Quotes within 1 business hour.
- Standard carrier liability topped up to invoice value on declared losses.
- Phone booking; proactive delay notifications.
- Dedicated point of contact.

## Premier
- 24x7 support.
- Quotes within 30 minutes during business hours, 2 hours after hours.
- Carrier liability topped up to 1.5x invoice value.
- Dedicated ops team; quarterly business reviews.
- Custom SOPs for high-value or unusual commodities.
