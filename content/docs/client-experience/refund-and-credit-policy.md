# Refund and Credit Policy

## When a credit is appropriate
- Service failure covered by an SOP (delay, damage, loss).
- Billing dispute resolved in the client's favor.
- Goodwill credit for a high-value relationship at the Director's discretion.

## Credit limits by role
- Account owner: up to 5% of the affected invoice.
- Director of Client Experience: up to 15%.
- VP of Operations: up to 30%.
- CEO: anything above.

## Process
1. Issue a credit memo with a clear reason, the affected invoice, and the
   authorizing role.
2. Apply the credit to the client's next invoice. Do not issue a refund check
   unless the client is leaving or specifically requests one.
3. Log the credit in the CRM so we can report on credit rate by client.

## What not to do
- Do not issue a credit without a documented reason.
- Do not stack credits (one credit per incident, no compounding).
