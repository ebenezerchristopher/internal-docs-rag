# Billing Dispute SOP

## When this applies
A client disputes any line item on an invoice: freight, fuel surcharge,
accessorial, demurrage, or storage.

## Steps

### 1. Triage within 1 business day
Confirm the disputed amount, the invoice number, and the client's specific
objection. Categorize: rate error, accessorial challenge, service failure,
duplicate charge, or "other".

### 2. Pull the supporting evidence
For rate errors: pull the original quote, the booked BOL, and the delivered
receipt. For accessorial challenges: confirm whether the accessorial was
disclosed on the quote and supported by the BOL annotations.

### 3. Decide
- Rate error or duplicate: full credit, no approval needed.
- Accessorial not disclosed: 50% credit, lead approval.
- Service failure: defer to the appropriate SOP (Delay, Damaged, Lost) and
  apply that remedy on top of the disputed amount.

### 4. Communicate the decision
Use the "billing-decision" template. Include the new invoice total, the credit
memo number, and the date the credit will appear on the client's statement.

## Service-level goal
Disputes resolved within 5 business days. Anything older than 10 business
days is escalated to Finance Director review.
