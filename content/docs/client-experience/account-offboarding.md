# Account Offboarding

## When a client cancels
1. Confirm the cancellation in writing; record the reason.
2. Run a final invoice reconciliation within 5 business days.
3. Hand off the client's commodity, lane, and SOP notes to the account
   successor (or archive if no successor).
4. Revoke TMS access 30 days after the final shipment.
5. Send an exit survey.

## Recoverable data
- Account notes and SOPs are retained for 7 years per the data retention
  policy.
- Operational data (BOLs, scans, claims) is retained per the same policy.

## When we cancel a client
Same as above, plus Legal sign-off and a 30-day written notice per the MSA.
