# Client Onboarding Checklist

## Pre-launch (T-7 days)
- [ ] Signed MSA and rate card on file.
- [ ] Credit application completed; PO terms approved by Finance.
- [ ] Primary, secondary, and escalation contacts exchanged.
- [ ] Cargo insurance certificate received (or waiver signed).
- [ ] Customs broker identified (for cross-border clients).
- [ ] TMS access provisioned for the client's shippers.

## Launch day (T-0)
- [ ] Welcome call held; 24/7 ops number shared.
- [ ] First shipment booked and confirmed in TMS.
- [ ] SOP for the client's specific commodities attached to their account
  notes (hazmat, oversized, temperature-controlled, etc.).

## First 30 days
- [ ] Weekly check-in for the first 4 weeks.
- [ ] Review the first 10 shipments with the client: on-time, exceptions,
  invoicing.
- [ ] Move the client to standard cadence at day 30 unless issues persist.
