# Damaged Freight SOP

## When this applies
A client reports visible damage, concealed damage, or shortage on receipt.

## Steps

### 1. Document within 24 hours
Request photos of the outer packaging, the inner packaging, the damaged goods,
and the bill of lading. Concierge claims where the client cannot photograph.

### 2. Open a carrier claim
File within the carrier's published window (typically 9 months for LTL, 5
business days for parcel). Convey the claim number to the client.

### 3. Decide the immediate remedy
If the goods are unusable and replacement is on a critical path, advance up to
50% of the invoice value to the client against a signed acknowledgment that
the advance will be netted against the eventual claim payout.

### 4. Communicate weekly
Send a status update every Friday until the claim is closed. Use the
"claim-update" template.

## Escalation
Escalate to the Director of Ops if the claim value exceeds $25,000 or if the
carrier denies liability within 30 days.
