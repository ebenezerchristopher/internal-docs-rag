# Lost Freight SOP

## When this applies
A shipment has not been delivered and the last scan is older than 48 hours,
or the carrier has declared the freight lost.

## Steps

### 1. Confirm with the carrier
Call the carrier's dispatch and request a trace. Get a trace reference number
in writing.

### 2. Decide: trace or declare lost
- If the last scan is < 14 days old: continue tracing, weekly check-ins with
  the carrier.
- If the last scan is >= 14 days old: open a formal loss claim and inform the
  client that the claim timeline has started.

### 3. Settlement
For declared losses, the carrier's liability cap is the lesser of $100 per
pound or the invoice value. The company tops up the difference up to the full
invoice value for clients on the "Plus" service tier, and up to 50% for
"Standard" clients.

### 4. Closeout
Update the client's account notes, refund any unused portion of the freight
charge, and request a post-incident review of the carrier.
