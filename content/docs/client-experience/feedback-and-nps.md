# Client Feedback and NPS

## Cadence
- NPS survey: 24 hours after a delivery is confirmed, only for clients on
  Plus or Premier tiers.
- Quarterly business review: Premier clients only.
- Annual relationship review: all clients.

## Scoring
- 0-6: detractor. Reach out within 1 business day; offer a call.
- 7-8: passive. Send a "thanks for the feedback" note.
- 9-10: promoter. Send a thank-you and ask for a referral if appropriate.

## How feedback flows
Survey responses are auto-imported into the CRM. Detractors create a
high-priority task for the assigned account owner. Promoters are flagged for
referral outreach.

## Internal review
NPS is reviewed monthly by the leadership team. Trending is tracked per
client and per commodity lane.
