# Incident Command

## Activation triggers
- A fatality or serious injury involving a company asset.
- A chemical spill, fire, or explosion at a company facility.
- A cybersecurity incident with material impact.
- A natural disaster affecting a major facility or lane.

## Roles
- Incident Commander: the senior leader on site or on call.
- Operations Chief: coordinates the operational response.
- Communications Chief: coordinates internal and external
  communications.
- Safety Officer: ensures the response itself is safe.

## Communication
- The first external communication is the company's standard holding
  statement.
- The Communications Chief approves any external communication
  before it goes out.
- The next of kin is notified in person, by the IC or a designated
  senior leader, before any public statement.

## After the incident
- After-action review within 14 days.
- Findings are documented and tracked to closure.
- The after-action report is shared with the leadership team and,
  where appropriate, the board.
