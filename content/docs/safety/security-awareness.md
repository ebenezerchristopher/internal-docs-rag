# Security Awareness

## Phishing
- Hover before you click. Verify the sender, the URL, and the
  context.
- When in doubt, forward the email to the security team and do not
  respond.
- A reported phishing email is the most common way the security
  team learns about a campaign.

## Passwords
- Use the password manager; do not reuse passwords.
- Enable MFA on every system that supports it.
- A passphrase of 4 unrelated words is stronger than a 12-character
  password with substitutions.

## Devices
- Lock your screen when you step away, even for a minute.
- Do not plug in unknown USB devices.
- Keep the operating system and browser up to date.

## Reporting
A suspected security incident is reported to the security team
within 1 hour. The security team triages and escalates as needed.
