# Vehicle Procurement

## Sourcing
Vehicles are sourced from a panel of approved dealers. The panel is
reviewed annually. New dealers are added only after a credit and
capability check.

## Spec
- Light duty: cargo van with a payload of at least 3,500 lb.
- Medium duty: 26 ft box truck with a liftgate.
- Heavy duty: day cab tractor with a 53 ft dry van trailer.

The spec is reviewed annually against the typical freight mix and the
fleet's mileage profile.

## Buy vs lease
- Vehicles under 100,000 miles/year: lease.
- Vehicles over 100,000 miles/year: buy.
- Reefer: lease only (technology changes quickly).

## Disposal
Vehicles are disposed of at 7 years or 250,000 miles, whichever comes
first. The disposal is documented and the title is transferred.
