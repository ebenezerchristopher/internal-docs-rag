# Driver Safety Bonus

## Eligibility
All full-time company drivers. Owner-operators are eligible if their
MVR and PSP scores meet the threshold.

## Measurement
- Quarterly preventable accident count.
- Quarterly HOS compliance rate.
- Quarterly customer feedback score (where available).

## Payout
- 0 preventable accidents, HOS >= 98%, feedback >= 4.5/5: $1,000.
- 1 preventable accident OR HOS between 95-98%: $500.
- 2+ preventable accidents OR HOS < 95%: $0.

## Disqualifying events
- A preventable fatality.
- A DUI.
- A positive drug or alcohol test.

## Payout cadence
Quarterly, with the check dated the 15th of the month following the
end of the quarter.
