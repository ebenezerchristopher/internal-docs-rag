# Tire Management

## Replacement intervals
- Steer tires: 50,000 miles or 3 years, whichever comes first.
- Drive tires: 80,000 miles or 4 years.
- Trailer tires: 100,000 miles or 5 years.

## Inspection
- Pre-trip: visual inspection for cuts, bulges, and tread depth.
- Quarterly: measured tread depth and pressure check.
- Annual: full inspection including the spare.

## Pressure
Tires are checked cold. The target pressure is on the door jamb
sticker. Under-inflation and over-inflation are equally
counterproductive.

## Re-tread
Drive and trailer tires are re-treaded once before disposal, if the
casing is in good condition. Steer tires are not re-treaded.
