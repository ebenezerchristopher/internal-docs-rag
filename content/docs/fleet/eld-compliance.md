# ELD Compliance

## Mandate
All commercial vehicles operating in the US use an ELD registered
with the FMCSA. Drivers carry the ELD manual and a copy of the
certification.

## Driver responsibilities
- Log in to the ELD at the start of the shift.
- Review and certify the log at the end of the shift.
- Annotate any unusual entries (e.g., personal conveyance,
  yard moves).
- Report ELD malfunctions within 8 hours.

## Company responsibilities
- Maintain the ELD registration.
- Retain ELD records for 6 months.
- Train drivers on the use of the specific ELD installed in their
  vehicle.

## Audits
ELD logs are audited monthly for HOS compliance. Drivers with
recurring violations receive targeted coaching.
