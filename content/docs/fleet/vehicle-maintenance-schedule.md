# Vehicle Maintenance Schedule

## Class intervals
- Light duty (vans, pickups): oil every 5,000 miles, tire rotation
  every 7,500 miles, full inspection every 15,000 miles.
- Medium duty (box trucks): oil every 10,000 miles, tire rotation
  every 15,000 miles, full inspection every 30,000 miles.
- Heavy duty (tractors): oil every 25,000 miles, tire rotation every
  50,000 miles, full inspection every 100,000 miles.

## DOT inspections
- Annual DOT inspection for every commercial vehicle.
- Pre-trip inspection by the driver before every shift.
- Post-trip inspection by the driver at the end of every shift.

## Out of service
A vehicle that fails an inspection is taken out of service until
repaired. The vehicle is not eligible for dispatch.

## Records
Maintenance records are kept in the fleet management system for the
life of the vehicle plus 1 year.
