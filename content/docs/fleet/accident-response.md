# Fleet Accident Response

## At the scene
1. Driver secures the scene, checks for injuries, and calls 911.
2. Driver exchanges information and photographs the scene.
3. Driver calls the driver manager (24/7 line).
4. Driver does not admit fault.

## After the call
1. Driver manager dispatches a tow if the vehicle is not drivable.
2. Driver manager notifies the insurance carrier.
3. Driver submits the accident report within 4 hours.
4. Driver submits to post-accident drug and alcohol test within 32
   hours.

## Vehicle
- Drivable: returned to the yard for inspection.
- Not drivable: towed to a panel repair shop.
- Total loss: declared by insurance, disposed of per the disposal
  policy.

## Recurring
A driver in two preventable accidents in 12 months is enrolled in
remedial training and reviewed for fitness for duty.
