# Permits and Oversize Loads

## When a permit is required
- Width over 8 ft 6 in.
- Height over 13 ft 6 in.
- Length over 53 ft (semi), 75 ft (combination).
- Weight over 80,000 lb gross.
- Any load that requires an escort.

## Lead time
Routine permits: 3-5 business days. Superloads: 10-15 business days.
Same-day permits: not offered.

## Routing
- Use the state-specific routing guide for the permit.
- Do not travel on restricted routes or during restricted hours.
- For multi-state moves, confirm the permit is valid in each state on
  the route.

## Escorts
- Width over 12 ft: front and rear escorts.
- Height over 14 ft 6 in: height-pole escort in addition to the
  front and rear.
- Superloads: state police escort, scheduled in advance.
