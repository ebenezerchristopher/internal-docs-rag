# Fuel Management

## Cards
Every company vehicle has a fuel card linked to the vehicle. Personal
use is prohibited. Lost or stolen cards are reported immediately.

## Pricing
- Card pricing is locked at the regional weekly average.
- Driver is responsible for selecting the cheapest accessible fuel
  within 5 miles of the planned route.

## Idling
Idling is limited to 5 minutes in any 60-minute period except in
extreme weather. Excessive idling is a coaching topic.

## Exceptions
- Refrigerated units: reefer may idle to maintain temperature.
- Emergency vehicles: idling permitted for emergency response.
